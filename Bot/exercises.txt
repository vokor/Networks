1|What? You are sleeping? I have something for you: Bodyweight Squat. 20 times will be great!
2|Now: Push-Ups. 15 times!!! There’s a reason push-ups are a go-to exercise for body builders. They effectively work the muscles in your shoulders and chest.
3|Mountain Climbers. 50 times) This exercise mimics the motion climbers make as they climb steep peaks, except it’s done on the soft, flat surface of your floor.
4|Plank. Don't be a vegetable, stand 2 minutes! A commonly seen exercise, plank helps build strength in the core, shoulders, arms and legs. Plank tones your abs and builds strength in your upper body.
5|Bodyweight Split Squat. 20 times for each leg. This variation on a squat really targets. Jumping into your starting position from the lowest point in your squat also adds a plyometric boost.
6|Single-Leg Hip Raise. Don't be a vegetable, do it 20 times for each leg! This exercise is adapted from yoga and targets the muscles in your glutes and abs.
7|Burpee With Push-Up. 50 times! Fun to say, but also great for your body, this total body exercise will get your heart pumping fast, but don’t sacrifice form for speed. Keep your body in control as you move through the exercise.
8|Single-Leg Toe Touches. 2 minutes) This exercise is a great way to tone your lower body. It helps to improve balance, while also targeting your hamstrings.
9|Leg Raises. I see you need something hard! 50 times! Build your core strength without getting off the floor. The legs down exercise is great for lower back pain, but be sure move your legs in a controlled manner throughout the exercise.
